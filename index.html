About me

Introduction 简介

I was born and raised here in the bay area, making Cal my dream school ever since I was a kid. College has a lot to offer, and I’m looking forward to all the classes and experiences in the years to come. I'm currently a sophmore studying computer science and cognitive science.

I am a 2nd degree black belt in martial arts, and I’ve taught fellow students self-defense for ten years. Martial arts is a great passion of mine, teaching me not only self defense, but also about discipline, leadership, and self-confidence. I have competed in many tournaments, including the World Championships in Las Vegas.

I really enjoy watching and playing sports, especially football (Go Niners!). Despite being a football enthusiast, my first football game that I attended in person was actually at Cal. Photography and graphic design are other big passions of mine. Mouse over to see "Landscape Bottle".
